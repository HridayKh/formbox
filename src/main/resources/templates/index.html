The route your data takes

Three stops between your visitor and your inbox

  1. 01

    Point your form

    Set the action attribute on any HTML form to your FormBox endpoint. No SDK, no npm install, no build step.

  2. 02

    We catch it at the edge

    Cloudflare's network receives and validates every submission close to your visitor, wherever in the world they are.

  3. 03

    It lands in your inbox

    Get pinged on Discord the moment someone submits — spam already filtered out before it reaches you.

Questions

Before you join the list

Do I need to change my HTML?

Barely. Add one action attribute pointing at your FormBox endpoint. That's the entire integration — the rest of your form stays exactly as you wrote it.

What happens when the public beta opens?

Everyone on the waitlist gets an invitation in signup order, along with an extended trial of whichever paid tier they reserved.

Is there a hard cap on submissions?

Each tier's submission count resets monthly. If your forms outgrow your plan, you can upgrade at any point — nothing you've already collected is affected.

Why Cloudflare's edge specifically?

Submissions are received by the data center nearest your visitor rather than a single origin server, so the round trip stays fast no matter where your traffic comes from.
