How Formbox Works

Three simple steps between your site visitor and your inbox.

01. Point your form

Set the action attribute on any HTML form to your Formbox endpoint URL. Works with HTML5, Next.js, Astro, Hugo, Eleventy, or Webflow.

02. Processed at the Edge

Submissions are caught at Cloudflare's global edge network. Honeypots, Turnstile, rate limits, and custom field validation rules filter out spam instantly.

03. Delivered to You

Receive notifications via Discord, Slack, Telegram, or custom Webhooks the moment a submission arrives, with attachments stored securely.

Frequently Asked Questions

Do I need to install any npm packages or SDKs?

No! Formbox requires zero client-side JavaScript or build dependencies. Simply set your HTML form's action attribute to your Formbox endpoint.

How does anti-spam protection work?

Formbox uses multi-layer defense: hidden honeypot fields, IP rate limiting, Cloudflare Turnstile, Altcha proof-of-work, and field validation rules.

Can I redirect visitors to my own thank-you page?

Yes, paid plans support custom redirect URLs so visitors are redirected to your custom page after submission.
